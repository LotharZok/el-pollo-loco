How To Play:

Walk Left Push Key A or Arrow Key Left or Number 4 on Numeric Keypad
Walk Right Push Key D or Arrow Key Right or Number 6 on Numeric Keypad
Jump Push Space Bar
Throw Bottle Push Key Q or Key E or Enter or Enter on Numeric Keypad
Collect Bottles and Coins by walking over them or jump to them when above
Kill chickens by jumping on them
Kill the large chicken a.k.a. the end boss by throwing at least 10 bottles on it
